# Home Page Suggestions

Based on the requirement: **"After login, the main home page will have a left sidebar, all blogs displayed in the center, and a suggestion section on the right sidebar."**

Here is a detailed breakdown of the proposed structure and features:

## 1. Left Sidebar (Navigation & Personalization)
*   **Purpose**: Quick access to core platform areas and user preferences.
*   **Components**:
    *   **Logo/Brand**: Top left.
    *   **Main Menu**:
        *   **Home/Feed**: The main dashboard.
        *   **Explore**: Discover new content/trending.
        *   **Bookmarks/Saved**: Saved articles.
        *   **History**: Recently read.
        *   **Drafts/Write**: Quick link to create a new blog.
    *   **Your Tags**: List of followed tags/categories.
    *   **User Profile Link**: Mini-profile at the bottom or top (Avatar + Name).
    *   **Settings**: Account settings link.

## 2. Center Section (The Feed)
*   **Purpose**: The primary content consumption area.
*   **Components**:
    *   **Tabs**:
        *   **"For You"**: Algorithmically sorted content based on interests.
        *   **"Following"**: Chronological posts from followed authors.
    *   **Blog Post Card**:
        *   **Header**: Author Avatar, Name, Timestamp.
        *   **Content**: Title (Bold), Brief Excerpt (2-3 lines), Cover Image (Right aligned or Top).
        *   **Footer**:
            *   Tags/Categories pills.
            *   "Read Time" (e.g., 5 min read).
            *   Like/Comment counts.
            *   Bookmark Icon.
    *   **Pagination**: Infinite scroll or "Load More" button.

## 3. Right Sidebar (Discovery & Suggestions)
*   **Purpose**: Promote engagement and discovery of new content/users.
*   **Components**:
    *   **"Suggested People" / "Who to Follow"**:
        *   List of 3-5 users with similar interests.
        *   "Follow" button next to each.
    *   **"Trending Topics"**:
        *   List of popular hashtags or categories (e.g., #AI, #WebDev).
    *   **"Top Picks"**:
        *   Curated list of 3 high-quality articles from the day/week.
    *   **Footer Links**: About, Help, Privacy, Terms.

## Design Aesthetics (Plan)
*   **Layout**: 3-Column Grid (e.g., 20% - 55% - 25%).
*   **Style**: Clean, minimalist, card-based feed.
*   **Responsiveness**:
    *   Desktop: 3 columns.
    *   Tablet: 2 columns (Left Sidebar + Center).
    *   Mobile: 1 column (Center only, with Hamburger menu for Sidebar).
